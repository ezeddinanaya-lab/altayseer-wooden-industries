About Us

Where Tradition Meets
Modern Craft

Al Tayseer Wooden Industries was born from a deep passion for the art of woodworking. For over three decades, we have been transforming raw timber into functional masterpieces that enrich homes, offices, and commercial spaces across the region.

Our workshop is home to artisans who carry generations of knowledge — blending time-honored joinery techniques with precision CNC machinery and contemporary design sensibilities.

  • ✓ Sustainably sourced premium hardwoods
  • ✓ Hand-finished with natural oils and lacquers
  • ✓ Full design-to-delivery service
  • ✓ Custom sizing and finishes available

Our Process

Craftsmanship is
Not a Step — It's Everything

Every piece that leaves our workshop passes through the hands of our artisans at each stage — never rushed, always refined.

01

Consultation & Design

We listen, sketch, and refine — translating your vision into detailed technical drawings and material specifications.

02

Material Selection

Hand-picking timber from sustainably managed forests. We inspect grain, moisture, and density before any cut is made.

03

Precision Cutting & Joinery

CNC precision meets hand-cut dovetails and mortise-and-tenon joints that will outlast generations.

04

Finishing & Quality Check

Hand-sanded through 7 grits, stained or oiled, and inspected against our zero-defect standard before delivery.
